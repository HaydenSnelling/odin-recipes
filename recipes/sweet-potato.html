Sweet Potato

Description

Sweet potatos are delicious and far superior to normal potatos. In this recipe you will learn how you can easily make perfect sweet potatos in your instant pot.

Ingredients

  • 2-4 medium-sized sweet potatoes (scrubbed and pierced with a fork)
  • 1 cup water
  • Optional: Salt, butter, cinnamon, or brown sugar for seasoning

Steps

  • Place the trivet (or a steamer basket) in the bottom of the Instant Pot insert.
  • Pour 1 cup of water into the Instant Pot.
  • Arrange the sweet potatoes on top of the trivet. You can stack them if necessary.
  • Close the Instant Pot lid and set the valve to the sealing position.
  • Set the Instant Pot to Manual or Pressure Cook on High for 15-20 minutes, depending on the size of the sweet potatoes.
  • Once the cooking time is complete, allow for a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure.
  • Carefully remove the sweet potatoes from the Instant Pot. Let them cool slightly before serving.
  • Optional: Top with butter, salt, cinnamon, or brown sugar for extra flavor.
