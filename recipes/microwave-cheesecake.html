Microwave Cheesecake

Description

This quick and easy microwave cheesecake is a perfect dessert when you're craving something sweet but don't want to spend hours in the kitchen. With a buttery graham cracker crust and creamy filling, it's an indulgent treat ready in just minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons butter (melted)
  • 8 oz cream cheese (softened)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup sour cream (optional, for extra creaminess)
  • Optional toppings: fresh berries, whipped cream, chocolate syrup, etc.

Steps

  • Start by preparing the crust. In a small bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Stir until the mixture is moist and crumbly.
  • Press the graham cracker mixture into the bottom of a microwave-safe mug or small bowl. Use the back of a spoon to compact it into an even layer. Microwave the crust for 30 seconds to set it, then set aside to cool.
  • In another bowl, beat the softened cream cheese with the granulated sugar and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy. You can use a hand mixer or a whisk.
  • Add the egg and beat again until the mixture is well combined. If using, fold in the sour cream for extra richness and creaminess.
  • Pour the cheesecake mixture over the prepared crust in the mug or bowl.
  • Microwave on high for 1 minute, then check the cheesecake. If the center is still slightly wobbly, continue microwaving in 20-second intervals until itâ€™s just set but still a little soft in the middle (total microwave time is usually around 1.5 to 2 minutes, depending on your microwave).
  • Let the cheesecake cool down to room temperature for a few minutes, then refrigerate for at least 1 hour to firm up further.
  • Once chilled, top with fresh berries, whipped cream, or your favorite topping. Serve and enjoy!
